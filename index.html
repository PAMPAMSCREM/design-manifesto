A Manifesto for Salt

Salt is Abundant
A currency of survival
a material of little value
it's potential so infinite
holding decay at a distance
it preserves what would rot
translucent grains
repeating patterns
so small to the eye
enduring heat, pressure and time.

Burney, Henna. "A Manifesto for Salt." designmanifestos. originally published on dezeen. 2021.
